Pendoria's New Direction

Jun 24, 2019 • Puls3

Endless Tower Is A No Go?

For quite a while the Developer of Pendoria, Maria, has been working on a feature known as The Endless Tower, however as of yesterday Xortrox (The Owner of Pendoria) released a “Small announcement” which I personally think is a big deal. Endless Tower is now postponed for an unknown time frame, which means that it could never come.

​

So What Now?

Maria has released what she would do after Endless Tower before, that is what she is working on now. She has an entire “Guild Rework” planned, which will include the following;

  • Custom Guild Ranks, which was suggested by me in month one while Zampa was still doing Q&As.
  • Guild Armories, which have long been promised by Xortrox.
  • Much more guild QoL which should help a ton.
  • Something related to making guild levels relevant, we don’t have much detail. All in all, Maria is now working on non-content changes to guilds, and many more QoL changes that should improve the overall quality of the game. Reactions to this were mixed however everyone seems ready to cheer on the updates outlined above.

​

Who is Evelyn?

Evelyn, the Admin that was just promoted from player to Admin less than 3 weeks ago, stepped down from his position which was defined as “admin/partial developer”. This came as much of a shock as his initial promotion did, I’m confused as to what this was all about, especially since Maria promised no more staff changes for a while.

​

Anything Else?

Nothing else has really changed besides a few little edits. A few QoL updates, some bug fixes, and no more please refresh pop-ups constantly. Also, you can now decide to look naked and take your title off, it looks weird. Final thing, if you invested your invasion points without thinking, you can now reset them the same way you can rhodium boosts.
