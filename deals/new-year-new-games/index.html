Steep Is Free!

Not too long ago I was telling players to download UPlay just to get Steep for free, well now another company has decided to provide this game free of charge. If you didn’t guess from how generous they’ve been recently, it’s Epic Games.

I’ve played quite a bit of Steep myself and can personally recommend it as a great game.

Until January 9th of 2020 (it feels weird saying 2020) it will be free on Epic Games;
- https://www.epicgames.com/store/en-US/product/steep/home

Darksiders
