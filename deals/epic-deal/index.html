Some Epic Deals

Dec 22, 2019 • Puls3

Free Game & $10 Coupon!

As usual Epic Games released their free game, there’s no real reason not to go pick it up. In fact it seems there’s even more of a reason than usual, as this time not only do you get the game, you also receive a $10 coupon! As usual there are limitations to using it, but either way the addition of it is a nice touch.

Here’s the limitations;

The $10 Epic Coupon can only be used on games already released on the Epic Games Store and priced at $14.99 or more after sale discounts have been applied. This coupon cannot be used on pre-purchases or any non-game purchase such as add-ons (like DLC or season passes) or in-game purchases (such as in-game currency, like Vbucks in Fortnite, or upgrades like Save the World mode).

A little thank you to my friend Ricardo for mentioning the limitations, if he hadn’t I would’ve forgotten to mention them.
